ingredients:
  - 4 tablespoons butter
	- 6 ounces oriental-flavor ramen
	- 3 tablespoons sesame seeds
	- ⅓ cup sugar
	- ⅔ cup white vinegar
	- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
	- 1 teaspoon pepper
	- ½ cup green onions
	- ¾ cup dry-roasted peanuts
	- 1 cup carrots
	- 2 pounds chicken
	- Cabbage
  - Scrambled egg (optional)
  - Edamame (optional)
preparations:
	- Cook and chop the chicken.
	- Chop the green onions.
	- Shred the carrots. 
directions:
	- Break the ramen into little pieces in its packet.
	- Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
	- Stir in seasoning packet from the noodles.
	- Add the ramen.
	- Cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
	- Stir in the sesame seeds.
	- Cook until noodles are golden brown (2 minutes).
	- Remove from heat and set aside. 
	- Mix the sugar, vinegar, oil, and pepper in a large bowl.
	- Add the green onions, peanuts, chicken, and enough shredded cabbage to make a good mix into to the bowl.
	- Toss in the noodle mixture.
