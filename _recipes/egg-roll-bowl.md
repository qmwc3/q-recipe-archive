title: "Egg Roll in a Bowl"
date: 2026-01-14
description: "A quick skillet meal with ground beef, cabbage, and classic egg roll flavors."
prep_time: "10 minutes"
cook_time: "20 minutes"
total_time: "30 minutes"
servings: "4"
tags:
  - dishes
source_name: ""
source_url: ""
ingredients:
  - "1½ pounds ground beef"
  - "1 yellow onion"
  - "1 tablespoon garlic"
  - "4 tablespoons sesame oil"
  - "2 cups carrots"
  - "16 ounces cabbage"
  - "½ teaspoon onion powder"
  - "½ teaspoon garlic powder"
  - "½ teaspoon red pepper flakes"
  - "1½ teaspoons ground ginger"
  - "½ cup soy sauce"
  - "3 stalks green onion"
  - "1½ cups rice"
preparations:
  - "Dice the onion."
  - "Shred the carrots."
  - "Cook the rice."
directions:
  - "Cook the ground beef in large skillet over medium heat until no longer pink, don't break up the ground beef yet."
  - "Drain the grease and return the skillet to the stovetop."
  - "Add the onion and garlic and break the ground beef; cook until the meat is browned."
  - "Add 2 tablespoons sesame oil, carrots, and cabbage to the skillet."
  - "Cook for 5 minutes."
  - "In a small bowl, combine onion powder, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, ground ginger, soy sauce, and 2 tablespoons sesame oil."
  - "Pour the sauce over the meat and cabbage mixture."
  - "Reduce heat and cook for an additional 5 minutes."
  - "Top with green onion."
  - "Serve over rice."
