ingredients:
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "1 teaspoon baking soda"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "¾ cup sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "⅓ cup orange juice"
  - "1 teaspoon grated orange rind"
  - "3 tablespoons white vinegar"
  - "½ cup water"
  - "¼ cup unsalted butter"
  - "1 cup cranberries"
  - "1 cup walnuts"
ingredients_subhead: ""
ingredients_2:
  - ""
preparations:
  - "Melt the butter."
  - "Chop the cranberries."
  - "Chop the walnuts."
directions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 350°F."
  - "Spray a loaf pan with non-stick spray."
  - "Sift the flour, baking soda, salt, and sugar into a medium bowl and whisk."
  - "In a large bowl, beat the egg and stir in the orange juice, orange rind, vinegar mixture, and butter."
  - "Add the flour mixture to the egg mixture and stir until just moistened."
  - "Add the cranberries and walnuts."
  - "Pour the batter into the loaf pan and sprinkle a few walnuts on top."
  - "Bake until done (1 hour and 10 minutes; 1 hour if cranberries were frozen)."
  - "Let the loaf cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack."
