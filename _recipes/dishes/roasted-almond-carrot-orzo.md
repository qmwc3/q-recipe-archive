ingredients:
  - "4 tablespoons olive oil"
  - "8 ounces orzo"
  - "2 teaspoons lemon zest"
  - "2 cups water"
  - "⅞ teaspoon salt"
  - "¾ teaspoon pepper"
  - "2 cups carrots"
  - "½ cup almonds"
  - "3 tablespoons lemon juice"
  - "1 teaspoon sugar"
  - "½ teaspoon dijon mustard"
  - "1 chicken breast"
ingredients_subhead: ""
ingredients_2:
  - ""
preparations:
  - "Chop the carrots."
  - "Cook and dice the chicken."
directions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 425°F."
  - "In a 10-inch skillet, heat 1 tablespoon olive oil over medium heat."
  - "Add the orzo and stir until toasted (2&nbsp;minutes)."
  - "Add 1 teaspoon lemon zest, 2 cups water, ½ teaspoon salt, and ¼ teaspoon pepper."
  - "Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until the liquid is absorbed (12&nbsp;minutes)."
  - "Place the carrots on a baking dish. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon olive oil, ¼ teaspoon salt, and ⅛ teaspoon pepper."
  - "Roast the carrots for 10 minutes."
  - "Remove the pan from the oven and add the almonds. Roast for another 5 minutes."
  - "In a large bowl, whisk together 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 teaspoon lemon zest, lemon juice, sugar, mustard, ⅛ teaspoon salt, and ⅛ teaspoon pepper."
  - "Add the chicken, roasted carrots, almonds, and cooked orzo to the dressing. Toss to coat."
