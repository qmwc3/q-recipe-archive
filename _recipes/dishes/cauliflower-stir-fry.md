ingredients:
  - 1 head cauliflower
  - 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  - 2 chicken breasts
  - ¼ cup roasted peanuts
  - 1 cup snow peas
  - 1 yellow bell pepper
  - 2 tablespoons ginger
  - 3 green onions
  - ⅓ cup Thai sweet chili sauce
  - 1½ teaspoons soy sauce
  - ¼ cup cilantro
  - 1 cup rice
ingredients_subhead: 
ingredients_2:
  - 
preparations:
  - Chop the cauliflower into bite-size florets.
  - Chop the green onion into 2-inch long pieces.
  - Chop the chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  - Prepare the rice.
directions:
  - Preheat the broiler.
  - Toss the cauliflower with 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, salt, and pepper.
  - Spread on a baking sheet and broil, stirring occasionally, until lightly charred and tender (5&nbsp;minute&nbsp;intervals).
  - Heat remaining 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  - Cook the chicken.
  - Add the peanuts and cook until lightly toasted (1&nbsp;minute), then remove to a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  - Add snow peas, bell pepper, and ginger to the skillet and cook until crisp-tender (1&nbsp;minute).
  - Add the green onion and broiled cauliflower.
  - Stir in the Thai sweet chili sauce and soy sauce, cooking until the vegetables are glazed (2&nbsp;minutes).
  - Top with toasted peanuts and cilantro.
  - Serve over rice.
