ingredients:
  - "1½ cups whole wheat flour"
  - "2 teaspoons cinnamon"
  - "1 teaspoon baking powder"
  - "1 teaspoon baking soda"
  - "¼ teaspoon nutmeg"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "¾ cup unsweetened applesauce"
  - "¼ cup honey"
  - "1½ teaspoon vanilla"
  - "1 cup fresh raspberries"
  - "⅓ cup walnuts"
  - "3 ounces dark chocolate"
ingredients_subhead: ""
ingredients_2:
  - ""
preparations:
  - "Chop the dark chocolate."
  - "Chop the walnuts."
  - "Wash and thoroughly dry the raspberries, then slice them in half."
directions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 350°F."
  - "Coat large muffin cups with cooking spray."
  - "In a large bowl, stir together the flour, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, and nutmeg."
  - "Make a hole in the center of the mixture."
  - "In a small bowl, lightly beat the eggs."
  - "Stir the applesauce, honey, and vanilla into the egg mixture."
  - "Add the egg mixture to the hole in the flour mixture."
  - "Stir until just moistened, still lumpy."
  - "Spoon a small amount of the batter into the bottom of each muffin tin."
  - "Fold in the raspberries, walnuts, and chocolate."
  - "Spoon the rest of the batter into the muffin dish so it’s ¾ full."
  - "Bake for 22 minutes or until done."
