ingredients:
  - 1 head of broccoli
  - 1 cup raisins or craisins
  - 1½ cups cashews
  - 1 cup cheddar cheese
  - ½ cup bacon bits
  - ½ cup slivered almonds
  - 2 stalks of green onion
  - 1½ cups mayonnaise
  - 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  - ¼ cup honey
  - 4 teaspoons sugar
  - 1 cup sunflower seeds
ingredients_subhead: ""
ingredients_2:
  - ""
preparations:
  - Chop the green onion.
  - Cut the broccoli head into bite-sized pieces.
directions:
  - Place the broccoli in a medium size bowl and add the raisins, cashews, cheese, bacon, almonds, and green onion.
  - Combine the mayonnaise, vinegar, honey, and sugar.
  - Add to the broccoli mixture.
  - Sprinkle the sunflower seeds over the broccoli and toss.
